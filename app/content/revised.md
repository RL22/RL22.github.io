## Who I Am

Hi — I'm Rodney L. Lewis, a senior web platform lead based in Oakland, CA. The last eight years have been the same job at different logos: take an inherited marketing site, treat it like product, and make the team faster.

At Pendo, Carrot Fertility, Kiddom, Andersen, and Revel Systems that meant reusable component systems, modular templates, *Core Web Vitals work*, A/B testing, technical SEO, and the publishing workflows that let marketing ship without an engineering ticket. At **Carrot, that work cut marketing dev requests by 40%**.

I work across the stack marketing actually touches — HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, REST APIs, React, WordPress and Webflow on the CMS side, Marketo, HubSpot, Mutiny, Salesforce, and Mailchimp on the mar-tech side.

*Beyond the day job, I run [Sprintz](https://sprintz.agency) — a hybrid practice that pairs high-converting websites with AI-native delivery systems. Same stack I'd recommend to any growth team: Next.js 16, Sanity CMS, Supabase, Cal.com, Playwright, Tailwind v4. The studio also drives my open-source work — the BMAD-OpenCode Engine (a Go-based multi-agent framework for methodology-aware growth-marketing workflows), readworthy, character.md, and antenna-os — and the Push to Prod podcast.*
> *Annotation:* the three-paragraph structure is intentional. P1 = positioning + owner verb ("make the team faster"). P2 = proof + resume number (bolded for scannability). P3 = stack (verified mar-tech list, no buzzwords). P4 = Sprintz/AI angle, all italic since none of it is on the resume.

# Projects — Case Studies

> *Card pattern: each project follows* **Context → Action → Outcome** *— the framework expected by senior hiring committees. Cards stay short enough for the grid; detail expands on click.*

### 1. Pendo.io — Marketing Site Modules (Sr. Web Developer, 2022–2023)
**Category:** Reusable Components
**Tags:** *Headless WordPress* · A/B Testing · SEO · Personalization

**Context:** Marketing needed to ship campaign pages without engineering bottlenecks while keeping data integrated for personalization and analytics.
**Action:** Built reusable landing-page modules and marketing templates *on a headless WordPress stack*. Partnered with demand gen on A/B tests and SEO. Shipped data-integrated components.
**Outcome:** Campaigns launched without an engineering ticket. Tests instrumented and reportable from day one.

### 2. Carrot Fertility — Marketing Website Lifecycle (Sr. Web Developer, 2021–2022)
**Category:** CMS Architecture & Performance
**Tags:** WordPress · Webflow · *Core Web Vitals* · Mobile-First

**Context:** Marketing site needed to ship faster on mobile, hold its rankings, and stop blocking marketing on dev time.
**Action:** Owned the marketing-site lifecycle end-to-end — *Core Web Vitals*, responsiveness, page speed, mobile-first performance. Built modular templates and publishing workflows in WordPress and Webflow.
**Outcome:** **Marketing dev requests dropped 40%.** Marketing self-served; engineering got their roadmap back.

### 3. Kiddom — Growth Components & Performance (Sr. Web Developer, 2021)
**Category:** SEO + Performance
**Tags:** SEO · Conversion · Lighthouse · UX

**Context:** Growth KPIs were stalling on a slow site with weak page-level SEO.
**Action:** Built SEO-optimized page components and conversion tooling aligned to growth KPIs. Partnered with UX and data on behavioral testing.
**Outcome:** Faster page loads, higher Lighthouse scores, growth components grounded in test data — not gut.

### 4. Andersen Digital — Migrations & B2B Campaigns (Web Developer, 2020–2021)
**Category:** *CMS & Mar-Tech Migrations*
**Tags:** *WordPress → Headless WP* · *Marketo → HubSpot* · A/B Testing · Lead Gen

**Context:** *Two enterprise clients needed to move off legacy stacks without losing traffic, lead capture, or campaign velocity.*
**Action:** *Led WordPress and mar-tech migrations — Rancher IO (Marketo → HubSpot) and Revel Systems (standard WordPress → headless WordPress, Marketo → HubSpot).* Also shipped WordPress themes, HubSpot landing pages, branded calculators, and lead-gen components for AppZen and Illumio. Technical SEO and A/B testing throughout.
**Outcome:** *Both clients shipped on the new stack with their data, rankings, and lead flow intact.*

### 5. Revel Systems — Marketing & Knowledge Center (Web Designer, 2016–2020)
**Category:** Marketing CMS
**Tags:** WordPress · Templates · UX · Retention

**Context:** Marketing site and knowledge center were dragging on retention — high bounce, hard to navigate.
**Action:** Built and maintained WordPress sites and custom templates for marketing and client education. Led optimization of training pages and the knowledge center.
**Outcome:** Lower bounce. Higher retention. A foundation the team could keep iterating on for years (*and a base I'd later return to migrate to headless WordPress via Andersen*).

### 6. *Sprintz — Founder Studio (2023–present, italicized — not on resume)*

**Tags:** *Next.js 16 · Sanity · Supabase · Tailwind v4 · Playwright · BMAD*

**Action:** *Built [sprintz.agency](https://sprintz.agency) on Next.js 16, Sanity CMS, Supabase forms, Cal.com booking, Playwright e2e, Tailwind v4 design system.
